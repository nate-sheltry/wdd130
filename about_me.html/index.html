Hello! my name is Nathaniel Dunham. I am 21 years-old, am single and have lived in three corners of the United States. Mainly: Vermont, Georgia, and Arizona. I've been a member of the Church my whole life, however I really started to take religion seriously once I started doing early morning seminary. The teachers and friends I had while in seminary changed the course of my life and showed me the blessings that came from choosing to live a Christ centered life. It would've been easy to go inactive as the only young man of my age group, but instead I chose to follow the examples of my great teachers from seminary!

People are my purpose in life. I don't like going to places, I like visiting people. Don't get me in a group though, yikes!
